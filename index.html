Phil started BJJ in 2012 and has been training under Professor Hoss from blue belt to brown belt. He is passionate about sharing his knowledge and experience with others, helping them improve their skills, fitness, and confidence. Every Friday he runs an early am class, and also assists with the tots program at Bairro. His own children's regular training in Muay Thai also keeps him plenty busy. Outside of martial arts, Phil dedicates some of his time to run a yoga class at an addictions recovery clinic, where patients learn to use relaxation to help with anxiety. Phil truly believes that BJJ is more than a sport, it is a lifestyle that promotes physical, mental, and spirtitual well-being.
