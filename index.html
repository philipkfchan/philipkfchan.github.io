Phil began his BJJ journey in 2012 and has been under the guidance of Professor Hoss,
advancing from blue to brown belt. His passion lies in exploring new techniques and
assisting others in enhancing their skills, fitness, and self-assurance. Whether he's
leading a class or supporting the tots program at Bairro, he remains actively engaged.
Additionally, he and his wife manage their children's training in Muay Thai. Beyond the
realm of martial arts, Phil devotes his time to introducing yoga to patients at a rehabilitation
clinic, aiding in their addiction recovery. Phil firmly believes that BJJ transcends mere
sportsmanship; it serves as a medium for diverse individuals to unite and apply the invaluable
lessons learned on the mats to their everyday lives.
